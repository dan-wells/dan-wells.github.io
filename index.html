I am a PhD student in the UKRI CDT in NLP in the School of Informatics at the University of Edinburgh. There I am a member of the Centre for Speech Technology Research and the Institute for Language, Cognition and Computation.

My research interests are in text-to-speech synthesis for low-resource languages, especially multilingual modelling, transfer learning and model adaptation in end-to-end neural network systems. My supervisor is Korin Richmond.
